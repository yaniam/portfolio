About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechatronics / Control and Automation Engineering from the University of Brasília (UnB), completed in 2023. This interdisciplinary program exposed me to a wide range of fields — including mechanics, electronics, computer science, and control systems — and ultimately led me to discover my passion for computer science, particularly in machine learning, data engineering, and computer vision.

My background across multiple branches of engineering has given me a strong foundation in analytical thinking, systems integration, and problem solving, as well as the ability to apply engineering principles to real-world challenges. This interdisciplinary experience allows me to bridge the gap between research and development, aligning teams and technologies toward common objectives.

I have hands-on experience in developing machine learning models, building data engineering pipelines, and designing computer vision systems for applied environments. My work often involves handling large datasets and transforming research concepts into scalable, production-ready solutions.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at UnB, where my research focuses on 3D data processing, particularly Point Cloud and Mesh analysis. In parallel, I work as an IT Analyst at Banco do Brasil, overseeing the management and maintenance of the bank's nationwide IT infrastructure — including ATMs, Queue Ticket Dispenser Terminals, and Customer Calling Display Terminals. My responsibilities include monitoring tens of thousands of devices, coordinating software updates, and troubleshooting operational issues to ensure seamless service delivery across the country.
